October Prize

Texas Trophy Hogs

With Haley Heath
Haley Heath, renowned huntress and Ford Outfitters TV host spends much of her time easing through the dark waters and dense forests of bayou country in search of chomping gators. A Perfect Weekend away from the gators for Haley would put her eye-to-eye with giant Texas Trophy Hogs. Haley wants you to experience this thrill too and has arranged for you to spend a weekend at Cotton Mesa Ranch in Corsicana TX with Petersen’s Hunting Executive Editor, Ben O’Brien, stalking the hills for truly giant hogs.


Haley’s Perfect Weekend includes 2 Night / 2 Days, All transportation, licenses, meals, fees and lodging at Cotton Mesa Ranch.
