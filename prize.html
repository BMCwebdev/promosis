August Prize

Leach Lake Walleye

When legendary guide and Ford Outfitters TV Host Fred Eichler tires of standing on a lone ridge in the coal-black darkness of early morning listening for the rasping scream of a bull elk shattering the silence he packs up and heads off to his idea of a Perfect Weekend off: fishing the dark still water for giant Minnesota Walleye with Jeff Simpson of In-Fisherman Magazine. Fred wants to send you on his Perfect Weekend with Jeff Simpson this summer. You’ll join Jeff and local guide and tournament veteran Jeff Andersen for 2 days on beautiful Leach Lake. Accommodations are at Chase on the Lake.


Fred’s Perfect Weekend includes 2 days/2 nights, all transportation, licenses, meals, fees and lodging at Chase-On-The-Lake.

September Prize

Montauk Stripers

Conway Bowman, Mako Shark Fly Fisherman pioneer and Ford Outfitters TV host knows a thing or two about line-peeling saltwater fun; after all, he takes on the Pacific in small boats, with skinny rods in search of the most powerful fish on earth. Conway’s Perfect Weekend is far to the east, chasing Stripers off Montauk in the fall with Fly Fisherman Magazines Editor Ross Purnell and friends. Conway has set up a Perfect weekend for you with Ross and local guide Captain Bryan Goulart. You’ll stay at Montauk Yacht Club and dine with Ross, Bryan and local legend and friend Paul Dixon, and fish for stripers off Montauks many beaches.


Conway’s Perfect Weekend includes 2 days/2 nights, all transportation, licenses, meals, fees and lodging at Montauk Yacht Club.

October Prize

Texas Trophy Hogs

Haley Heath, renowned huntress and Ford Outfitters TV host spends much of her time easing through the dark waters and dense forests of bayou country in search of chomping gators. A Perfect Weekend away from the gators for Haley would put her eye-to-eye with giant Texas Trophy Hogs. Haley wants you to experience this thrill too and has arranged for you to spend a weekend at Cotton Mesa Ranch in Corsicana TX with Petersen’s Hunting Executive Editor, Ben O’Brien, stalking the hills for truly giant hogs.


Haley’s Perfect Weekend includes 2 days/2 nights, all transportation, licenses, meals, fees and lodging at Cotton Mesa Ranch.

December Prize

Georgia Quail

Hollywood actor, hunting superstar and Ford Outfitters TV Host, Huntley Ritter spends a lot of time in front of cameras, working endless hours to entertain us. But when the lights go dark and it’s time to get away his Perfect Weekend is busting coveys of Georgia Quail and letting the dogs do the work. Huntley has set up a Perfect Weekend for you just outside Atlanta with Game & Fish Magazine Publisher Pete Gross. You’ll hunt with Pete at a Members Only plantation where the birds are fast and the hospitality warm. And at the end of the day you can relax at beautiful Barnsley Gardens Resort.


Huntley’s Perfect Weekend includes 2 days/2 nights, all transportation, licenses, meals, fees and lodging at Barnsley Gardens.
