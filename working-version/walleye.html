August Prize

Leach Lake Walleye

With Fred Eichler
When legendary guide and Ford Outfitters TV host Fred Eichler tires of standing on a lone ridge in the coal-black darkness of early morning listening for the rasping scream of a bull elk shattering the silence he packs up and heads off to his idea of a Perfect Weekend off: fishing the dark still water for giant Minnesota Walleye with Jeff Simpson of In-Fisherman Magazine. Fred wants to send you on his Perfect Weekend with Jeff Simpson this summer. You’ll join Jeff and local guide and tournament veteran Jeff Andersen for two days on beautiful Leach Lake. Accommodations are at Chase on the Lake.


Fred’s Perfect Weekend includes two night / two days, all transportation, licenses, meals, fees and lodging at Chase-On-The-Lake.
